# Summary

Potential benefits of intended AI system functionality and performance are examined and documented.

# Subcategories

- [MAP 3.1](../subcategories/map-3-1.md) - Potential benefits of intended AI system functionality and performance are examined and documented.
- [MAP 3.2](../subcategories/map-3-2.md) - Potential costs, including non-monetary costs, which result from expected or realized AI errors or system functionality and trustworthiness - as connected to organizational risk tolerance - are examined and documented.
- [MAP 3.3](../subcategories/map-3-3.md) - Targeted application scope is specified and documented based on the system’s capability, established context, and AI system categorization.
- [MAP 3.4](../subcategories/map-3-4.md) - Processes for operator and practitioner proficiency with AI system performance and trustworthiness – and relevant technical standards and certifications – are defined, assessed and documented.
- [MAP 3.5](../subcategories/map-3-5.md) - Processes for human oversight are defined, assessed, and documented in accordance with organizational policies from GOVERN function.
