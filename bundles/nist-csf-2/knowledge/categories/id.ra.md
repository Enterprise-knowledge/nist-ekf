# Summary

The cybersecurity risk to the organization, assets, and individuals is understood by the organization

# Structure

This Category is part of `ID` and contains 10 active Subcategories.

## Subcategories

- [ID.RA-01](../subcategories/id.ra-01.md) - Vulnerabilities in assets are identified, validated, and recorded
- [ID.RA-02](../subcategories/id.ra-02.md) - Cyber threat intelligence is received from information sharing forums and sources
- [ID.RA-03](../subcategories/id.ra-03.md) - Internal and external threats to the organization are identified and recorded
- [ID.RA-04](../subcategories/id.ra-04.md) - Potential impacts and likelihoods of threats exploiting vulnerabilities are identified and recorded
- [ID.RA-05](../subcategories/id.ra-05.md) - Threats, vulnerabilities, likelihoods, and impacts are used to understand inherent risk and inform risk response prioritization
- [ID.RA-06](../subcategories/id.ra-06.md) - Risk responses are chosen, prioritized, planned, tracked, and communicated
- [ID.RA-07](../subcategories/id.ra-07.md) - Changes and exceptions are managed, assessed for risk impact, recorded, and tracked
- [ID.RA-08](../subcategories/id.ra-08.md) - Processes for receiving, analyzing, and responding to vulnerability disclosures are established
- [ID.RA-09](../subcategories/id.ra-09.md) - The authenticity and integrity of hardware and software are assessed prior to acquisition and use
- [ID.RA-10](../subcategories/id.ra-10.md) - Critical suppliers are assessed prior to acquisition
