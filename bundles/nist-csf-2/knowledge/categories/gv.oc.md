# Summary

The circumstances - mission, stakeholder expectations, dependencies, and legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements - surrounding the organization's cybersecurity risk management decisions are understood

# Structure

This Category is part of `GV` and contains 5 active Subcategories.

## Subcategories

- [GV.OC-01](../subcategories/gv.oc-01.md) - The organizational mission is understood and informs cybersecurity risk management
- [GV.OC-02](../subcategories/gv.oc-02.md) - Internal and external stakeholders are understood, and their needs and expectations regarding cybersecurity risk management are understood and considered
- [GV.OC-03](../subcategories/gv.oc-03.md) - Legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements regarding cybersecurity - including privacy and civil liberties obligations - are understood and managed
- [GV.OC-04](../subcategories/gv.oc-04.md) - Critical objectives, capabilities, and services that external stakeholders depend on or expect from the organization are understood and communicated
- [GV.OC-05](../subcategories/gv.oc-05.md) - Outcomes, capabilities, and services that the organization depends on are understood and communicated
