# Summary

Access to physical and logical assets is limited to authorized users, services, and hardware and  managed commensurate with the assessed risk of unauthorized access

# Structure

This Category is part of `PR` and contains 6 active Subcategories.

## Subcategories

- [PR.AA-01](../subcategories/pr.aa-01.md) - Identities and credentials for authorized users, services, and hardware are managed by the organization
- [PR.AA-02](../subcategories/pr.aa-02.md) - Identities are proofed and bound to credentials based on the context of interactions
- [PR.AA-03](../subcategories/pr.aa-03.md) - Users, services, and hardware are authenticated
- [PR.AA-04](../subcategories/pr.aa-04.md) - Identity assertions are protected, conveyed, and verified
- [PR.AA-05](../subcategories/pr.aa-05.md) - Access permissions, entitlements, and authorizations are defined in a policy, managed, enforced, and reviewed, and incorporate the principles of least privilege and separation of duties
- [PR.AA-06](../subcategories/pr.aa-06.md) - Physical access to assets is managed, monitored, and enforced commensurate with risk
