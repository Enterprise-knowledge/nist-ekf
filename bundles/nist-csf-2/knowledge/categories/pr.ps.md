# Summary

The hardware, software (e.g., firmware, operating systems, applications), and services of physical and virtual platforms are managed consistent with the organization's risk strategy to protect their confidentiality, integrity, and availability

# Structure

This Category is part of `PR` and contains 6 active Subcategories.

## Subcategories

- [PR.PS-01](../subcategories/pr.ps-01.md) - Configuration management practices are established and applied
- [PR.PS-02](../subcategories/pr.ps-02.md) - Software is maintained, replaced, and removed commensurate with risk
- [PR.PS-03](../subcategories/pr.ps-03.md) - Hardware is maintained, replaced, and removed commensurate with risk
- [PR.PS-04](../subcategories/pr.ps-04.md) - Log records are generated and made available for continuous monitoring
- [PR.PS-05](../subcategories/pr.ps-05.md) - Installation and execution of unauthorized software are prevented
- [PR.PS-06](../subcategories/pr.ps-06.md) - Secure software development practices are integrated, and their performance is monitored throughout the software development life cycle
